Master your Workflow

Three ways to interact with your data.

01

The Dashboard

Sign in with your Lou Hau credentials. Use the Folder Tree in the sidebar for rapid nesting or double-click any folder to dive in. Switch between List and Grid modes depending on your tasks.

02

Quick Uploads

Need to upload fast? Drag files directly from Finder onto the System Tray cloud icon. The menu stays open until you're done, acting as a persistent drop-target for your most important documents.

03

Seamless Sharing

Right-click any file to copy a public share link. To download a shared file, click the Link Icon in the toolbar and paste any LHDrive share link—the app handles the rest, including file typing.
